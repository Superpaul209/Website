    • THE CITY MUST SURVIVE


    Frostpunk is a society survival game where heat means life and
    every decision comes with a cost. You face the task of
    building the last city on Earth and securing the means necessary
    for your people to survive

    MAINTAIN HOPE


    Survival is about hope and the will to live. Your ability to spark and maintain them both in your people will be a
    deciding factor in your success.


    MAKE THE LAW


    Society is a group of people who abide by the same rules and
    share similar beliefs. Establish laws that regulate the
    existence of your growing society. Decide on their working
    routine, healthcare, food provision and other crucial aspects of
    everyday life. Maintain their hope and contentment – the
    moral condition of your society is as important as securing
    the basic means to keep them fed and safe
